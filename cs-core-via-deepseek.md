## ðŸš€ Immediate Action Plan

### Semester 1-2: Foundation
1. **Complete 2-3 online courses** in mathematical foundations
2. **Read 1 research paper weekly** with structured analysis
3. **Build a technical portfolio** with 2-3 small projects
4. **Establish relationships** with 2-3 professors in your department

### Semester 3-4: Execution
1. **Join a research group** or start independent project
2. **Submit to student competitions** (ACM/IEEE SRC)
3. **Attend 1-2 conferences** (virtual or in-person)
4. **Develop domain specialization** in chosen area

### Semester 5-6: Contribution
1. **Aim for first publication** (workshop, demo, or full paper)
2. **Practice peer review** through journal clubs and shadow reviews
3. **Present work at conferences**
4. **Mentor junior students** in research methods
